Japan has one of the most advanced IT and communication markets in the world, and as the capital, Tokyo is at the forefront. Home to many world-renowned electronics brands, Tokyo has been at the advent of new technologies for many years as a place of early adopting consumers. They were at the forefront of the early take up of mobile communications, personal entertainment systems and computer electronics. Here are some of the top tech companies in Tokyo.

FPT Software

FPT Software is a part of FPT Corporation - the global leading technology and IT services group headquartered in Vietnam with nearly US $1 billion in revenue and 28,000 employees. The company has worked on over 11,000 outsourcing projects in the last 19 years and has a broad presence in diverse global markets.

Square Enix

Square Enix is a video game developer, publisher, and distribution company headquartered in Shinjuku, Tokyo. The company operates a global network of leading development studios and boasts a valuable portfolio of intellectual property, including: FINAL FANTASY, DRAGON QUEST, and TOMB RAIDER. Square Enix values innovation & creativity, and pride themselves on creating a corporate culture that is both collaborative and competitive.

Lookout

Lookout is a cyber security company that makes it possible for individuals and enterprises to be both mobile and secure. The Lookout security cloud can identify connections that would otherwise go unseen -- predicting and stopping mobile attacks before they do harm. This is the only mobile threat defense vendor to be named to Forbes Cloud 100 for four consecutive years. Lookout preaches that diveristy is fundamental for them, stating that employees from diverse backgrounds bring the individual skills and experience needed for success.
